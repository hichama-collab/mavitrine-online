Consultation FAQs

Common questions about our consultation process.

How long is the consultation?

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes, giving us enough time to understand your needs and provide meaningful insights.

What should I bring to the consultation?

Please bring recent investment statements, tax returns, insurance policies, and any financial documents that provide a complete picture of your current situation.

Is there any cost for the consultation?

No, the initial consultation is completely complimentary. We believe in demonstrating our value before asking for your business.

What happens after the consultation?

We'll provide you with a detailed proposal outlining our recommended strategy, fee structure, and next steps. You'll have time to review everything before making any decisions.
